About Us

Caring for Cars Since 1996

Welshpool Autofit is a trusted, independent garage proudly serving the Welshpool area for nearly three decades. Run by Colin Davies, a lifelong car enthusiast with a special passion for American vehicles.

As a Class 4 MOT station, we are fully equipped to handle a wide range of vehicles - including modified, lowered, and performance cars, thanks to our recessed lift designed to accommodate specialist setups.

With nearly 30 years of hands-on experience as both an MOT tester and garage owner, Colin brings extensive knowledge, attention to detail, and genuine care for every vehicle that comes through our doors.
