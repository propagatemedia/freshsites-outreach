About Us

A Garage You Can Trust

Graig Goch Garage in Cwmbach, Builth Wells has been serving the local community with reliable vehicle servicing and MOT testing for years.

We provide full workshop facilities with competitive prices and a local collection and delivery service for your convenience. No job is too large or too small.

As a DVSA approved MOT testing station, we test classes 4 and 7 including cars, vans and goods vehicles up to 3,500kg design gross weight.
