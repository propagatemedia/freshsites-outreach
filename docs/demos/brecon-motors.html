About Us

A Family Business for 40+ Years

Brecon Motors is a family-run business in the heart of the Brecon Beacons, providing a diverse selection of quality used cars for over 40 years.

Jonathan has over 40 years experience in the retail motor trade, and his daughter Sara has been here for the last 13 years. We specialise in carefully selected QUALITY LOW MILEAGE used vehicles.

We pride ourselves on exceptional customer service, professionalism, and a friendly approach. We know buying a car can be daunting - so we go the extra mile to ensure you get the advice you need.
